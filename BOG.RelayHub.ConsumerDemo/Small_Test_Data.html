All Draw game prizes must be claimed at a Florida Lottery retailer or Florida Lottery office on or before the 180th day after the winning drawing.

Machine and Ball Sets

Machines for Florida Lottery draw games are replaced as needed based on usage and maintenance requirements. Ball sets for all Florida Lottery draw games are replaced at predefined intervals ranging from 2 to 3 years.
